STUDENTS' CREATIVE SUBMISSIONS

The 40th Anniversary celebration will showcase current students' creative work that celebrates computer science and diverse computing at Wellesley for a chance to win prizes!

Some creative ideas to help you get started:

Create an engaging video, podcast, or other form of media about an interview with an alum. Email eni.mustafaraj@wellesley.edu for a list of approved alums and more info.

A lip-sync video on the theme of computing, similar to this group of CS Faculty lip-syncing to Call Me Maybe .

Infographics / posters / IG stories / TikTok videos about the growth of CS, Media Arts / Sciences, Data Science, or other aspects of computing at Wellesley.

An interactive game, website, or comic book that centers the experience of being a student at Wellesley.

A website about computing history at Wellesley (like this project by Sunnia Ye '17) or a website about the accomplishment of our CS & MAS alums (like Wellesley in Tech)

Any other computing-inspired projects: jewelry, Halloween costumes, tattoos, stickers, commemorative pins, buttons, etc.
